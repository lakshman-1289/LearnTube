# 📚 LearnTube – AI Powered Dynamic Course Generator

## 🚀 Project Overview

**LearnTube** is an AI-powered platform that converts educational YouTube videos into structured learning courses.

Many students rely on YouTube for learning, but the content is often unstructured and lacks documentation, assessments, and progress tracking.

LearnTube aims to transform free educational videos into organized learning experiences by automatically generating modules, notes, and assessments from video transcripts.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Students who learn through YouTube face several challenges:

* No structured syllabus
* No organized notes or documentation
* No assessments to test understanding
* No progress tracking
* No certification for completed learning

Although YouTube contains valuable educational content, it lacks the structure of professional learning platforms.

---

## 💡 Proposed Solution

LearnTube allows a user to:

1. Enter a **YouTube video URL**
2. Extract the **video transcript**
3. Use **AI processing** to convert the transcript into:

   * Structured learning modules
   * Key concepts
   * Summaries
   * Learning notes
4. Generate **practice questions or assessments**
5. Provide a **certificate upon completion**

This enables students to convert **free video content into structured courses**.
